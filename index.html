Marine predator research that makes a difference.

We study Southern Ocean and other marine predators to improve our understanding of the natural world and help inform conservation and management decision-making.

Our Research Themes

  • Ecology & Behaviour

    Investigating the hunting, mating, and social behaviours of marine predators to understand their roles in marine ecosystems.

  • Anthropogenic Interactions

    Examining the impact of human activities such as fishing, plastic pollution, and invasive species on marine predators and their habitats.

  • Drivers of Behaviour

    Studying the environmental and biological factors that influence marine predators’ behaviours, such as migration, foraging, and diving.

  • One Health

    Exploring the connections between the health of marine predators, their habitats, and human health.

  • Ecosystem Monitoring

    Monitoring the health of marine ecosystems and the animals that live in them.
